Symptoms

Generally symptoms are not shown explicitly. Some of them are,

High BP and Chest pain

High blood pressure is the major symptom of kidney disease

Swelling of legs and ankels

Swelling of legs occurs because of Diabetes mellitus

Urinating more or less

The presence of creatinine in blood sample indicates the CKD

Causes of Chronic Kidney Disease

Type 1 or type 2 diabetes

Diabetes is the leading cause of kidney failure

Glomerulonephritis

An inflammation of the kidney's filtering units

Vesicoureteral

A condition that causes urine to back up into your kidneys
